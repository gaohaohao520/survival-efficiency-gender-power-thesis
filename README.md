## 论文核心摘要 | Core Abstract
本文通过物种竞争与生产模式演变的逻辑，论证父系权力起源是人类生存效率的自然选择，而非刻意压迫，为当代性别对立提供客观认知基础。
This paper demonstrates that the origin of patrilineal power is a natural choice of human survival efficiency rather than deliberate oppression through the logic of species competition and the evolution of production modes, providing an objective cognitive basis for contemporary gender conflict.
